About Us

The Catalyst Club at VIT Pune is dedicated to fostering innovation and entrepreneurship among students. We provide a platform for students to enhance their skills, collaborate on projects, and connect with industry professionals.

Who We Are

We are a team of passionate students who believe in the power of innovation and creativity. Our members come from diverse academic backgrounds and share a common goal of promoting entrepreneurial spirit within the campus.

Our Initiatives

  • Workshops: We organize hands-on workshops on topics like coding, design thinking, and business development.
  • Hackathons: Our hackathons challenge students to create innovative solutions to real-world problems within a limited timeframe.
  • Networking Events: We host events where students can meet industry experts and learn about various career opportunities.
